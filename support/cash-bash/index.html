Cash Bash

Cavalier Cash Bash will be Saturday, Feb. 15, 2020, from 6:30-11 p.m., at the Quality Inn, 800 N. Kinzie Ave., in Bradley.

There's a $5,000 grand prize, plus 23 other cash prize winners. There will be plenty of raffle baskets, too!

Tickets are $100. Each ticket admits two people for drinks and a food buffet. Must be 21 or older to attend. Only 300 tickets will be sold. Winners need not be present.

For information and tickets, call 815-802-8600.

Print the form, complete it, and mail it in with payment.
