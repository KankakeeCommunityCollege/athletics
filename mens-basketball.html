Chris Gardner is in his second season as head coach for the Cavaliers. In his first season, he led the Cavaliers to a 22-10 record, along with breaking two school records in three pointers made in a single game (17) and three pointers in a single season (294).

Donnie Gardner Sr.
Assistant Coach

Assistant Coach Donnie Gardner Sr. is in his first season with the Cavaliers. Gardner has an extensive background working with youth in multiple sports. He coached and mentored youth from ages 5-18 in the Youth Basketball Association from 1986-99, he was an assistant at Beasley Academic Center from 1994-96, and he coached Little League Baseball from 1987-1996 with South Side Little League and Jackie Robinson West. Coach Gardner also served as attendance coordinator at Whitney Young High School from 1996-2001.
