 Season Preview

Last season, the Cavs finished the season with a 21-11 record.

This season, the Cavaliers will feature five freshmen and nine sophomores. As a team the Cavs want to excel on the basketball court, in the classroom and in the community.

Each season, the Cavs bring in top talent from the Kankakee area and around the Midwest. This year’s team has players from Illinois, Kentucky, Indiana and Ohio.

The coaching staff and players are excited to embark on the 2021 season. The focus continues to improve the culture of KCC men’s basketball. Please join us through live streaming for as many home games as possible. We truly believe you will not be disappointed!
