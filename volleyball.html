 Season Preview

The Cavaliers volleyball team enters the 2021 season with new coaching staff, four returning players and nine new faces. Through the changes, the team has adapted well. They are using these changes as opportunities to grow, compete and achieve greatness.

“Going into this season as a new coach, there are a lot of different emotions," said Head Coach Jennifer Hoekstra. "I feel this team has the potential to rise up to any challenges that are set before them.”Coach Hoekstra is in her first year as head coach, and is joined by Assistant Coach Jennifer Ostrowski. They bring a lot of experience and great energy to the team."We go into the season with a lot of great talent and a team that is not afraid of hard work," Hoekstra said.. "We are here to 'Rise together because greatness is limitless.'”
