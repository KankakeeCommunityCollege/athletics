 Season Preview

The Lady Cavaliers are entering the 2021 season with high expectations. The team has seven returning players, including four starters from last season. Six new players will join the team, filling some key positions for future success.

The Lady Cavaliers look to start off the season strong with leadership from Setter Anna Jennings (co-captain) and Libero Ariana Grund. Rylee Panozzo (co-captain), Brandi Chase and Shaina Falkner will lead the way in the front line. Megan Berns will be playing through the back, after leading the team in serving percentage in 2020.

Dee Davis was out most of the 2020 season with a knee injury. She has worked hard in the off-season to return to the court.

The incoming freshmen will fill in a couple spots where the Cavaliers need extra strength.

The team is adding two assistant coaches for 2021, both bringing a lot of experience. Jennifer Hoekstra was a front row player for the University of Utah, a PAC-12 Conference team. She then coached at Bradley-Bourbonnais Community High School for five years. Jennifer Ostrowski (’14) was also a front row player at KCC and finished playing in the NAIA at Peru State College in Nebraska. Their experience will help this team excel in 2021.
