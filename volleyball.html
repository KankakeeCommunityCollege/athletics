 Season Preview

The Cavaliers volleyball team enters the 2022 season with a new head coach, six returning players and five new faces. The team has faced a lot of adversity already as a team, but have adapted well and is overcoming obstacles a lot of teams don't have to endure their first week.

"When you raise the level of expectations, true athletes will rise up and meet or exceed them,” Coach Thompson said. “The key factors in driving your success and building a sound foundation for your future, are consistency, accountability, respect, discipline and structure." Coach Thompson is in her first year at KCC, and is assisted by Coach Jennifer Ostrowski. They bring a lot of knowledge, passion and energy to the game and are excited to be working together this season.
