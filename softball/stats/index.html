KCC Softball Program History

The KCC women's softball team won the national title in 2015. Coach Dennis Clark led the Cavaliers to a 5-1 victory in the title game over East Central Community College of Decatur, Miss. The Cavaliers, who play in Division II of the NJCAA, finished the season with a 45-6 record. It was the first national title for the team and for Clark, who was inducted in to the NJCAA Softball Hall of Fame in December 2014.

KCC has participated in every NJCAA Division II softball national tournament since 2000.
