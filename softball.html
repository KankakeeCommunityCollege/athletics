 Season Preview

The 2025 Cavaliers softball program is coming off a 43-8 season in 2024, a season best record under Coach Cooper.

They look to make their mark on the 2025 season with a schedule that includes Region 23 powerhouses of Jones College, Pearl River, Mississippi Gulf Coast, and Copiah-Lincoln; Region 12’s Lansing, Region 8’s D-I Pensacola State; Region 16’s St. Louis; Region 24’s Parkland and Danville; as well as Region 4 rivals Rock Valley, Madison, Bryant & Stratton, and Triton.

The team is balanced between experience and fresh talent, with nine sophomores and nine freshmen. As winners of 24 of the last 32 Region IV Championships, the Cavs expect to be a strong contender to win their 25th NJCAA D-II Region 4 Championship and make a trip to the National Tournament.

The Cavaliers will depend on the strong play and leadership of the sophomores and look to the freshmen to quickly learn and gain experience to contribute.

With dependable hitting, power, and speed as well as some top returning pitching, the Cav’s are expected to compete in a tough Region IV once again.
