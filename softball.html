 Season Preview

Fresh off a stellar 44–16 campaign in 2025, the Lady Cavs enter the 2026 season with momentum, confidence, and unfinished business. The program is poised to build on a strong run in Region 4, and continue establishing itself as a premier contender.

While last season’s accomplishments set a high bar, this year’s squad will have a new look.

The Lady Cavs welcome 14 newcomers, bringing energy, versatility, and competition to every position on the diamond. With so many fresh faces, early-season chemistry and development will be key, but the talent level suggests the transition could be an exciting one.

Anchoring the team will be a strong core of sophomores who understand the expectations, the grind of a full season, and what it takes to compete deep into postseason play. They will provide stability and help integrate newcomers into the program’s culture and system.

The 2026 Lady Cavs may feature new names, but the standard remains the same. Led by experienced sophomores and a dynamic incoming class, the team carries momentum from a 44-win season. Focused on taking the next step in Region 4, they are ready to turn potential into another memorable spring.
