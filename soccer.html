 Season Preview

The Cavaliers enter the 2020-21 campaign with high expectations amid all the challenges resulting from the COVID-19 disruptions. 

“With the changes to our season start date, as well as limited contact with the team, the coaching staff has stepped up and made the necessary adjustments to prepare for what will be a challenging campaign,” Coach Barclay states. “We have redesigned our training during our off-season to make sure we are ready to hit the ground running come the season. The shortened schedule will make each game even more important to reach our team goals. As a coaching staff, we are extremely proud of the accomplishments the program has made over the past six years, but we still have lots of work to do. Every year our players have seen the accomplishments of the previous team and set their goals to continue this upward momentum. I am confident that this year will not disappoint.”
