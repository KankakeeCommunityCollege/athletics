 Season Preview

The Cavaliers enter the season with high hopes, returning a core group of players from last year’s campaign and a high-quality freshmen recruiting class.

“The incoming freshmen are one of the most talented groups we’ve seen in a long time and bring added dimensions to what we are going to be able to do this year,” said Head Coach Mike Barclay. “We’ve filled some important gaps in our lineup and are looking to bring a consistent game-in and game-out performance this year.”
