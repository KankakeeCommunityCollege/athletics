 Season Preview

The KCC men’s basketball program will have a new look this season. First year Head Coach Michael Brown has brought in student-athletes from five states, plus two international recruits. “We targeted guys that were the right fit for our program and our institution. It is a group that brings experience, work ethic, and a winning mentality. With continued hard work and playing together, we believe we will accomplish our goals this season.” Brown said.  


With the COVID eligibility rule, this year’s team will feature 8 freshman and 4 sophomores. There are 3 returners from last year’s team. 6’0 guard Chris Roberts (Chicago, IL) is an explosive player who averaged 9.8 points and 3.1 rebounds while shooting 55% from the field last year. 6’8 forward Kevin Vance (Chicago Heights, IL) is an extremely athletic player that will strengthen the interior. 6’6 forward Robert Stroud (Danville, IL) is an athletic do-it-all forward that will help the Cavs again this season.

There are nine new faces to the Cavs roster.  5’9 guard Amiri Young (Chicago, IL), a transfer from Otero Junior College, played 2 seasons ago at Region IV member Daley College where he averaged 21 points and 5 assists per game. NEO A&M transfer 6’5 guard James Franklin Jr. (Murfreesboro, TN) is an extremely athletic and versatile wing that averaged 9.2 points and 3.5 rebounds per game last season. Sophomore 6’3 guard David Perkins (Indianapolis, IN) is a smooth multi-positional player that has the ability to affect the game in a variety of ways.  6’4 guard Isaiah Placide (Atlanta, GA), a transfer from McLennan Community College, brings outside shooting and toughness to the program.

6’5 forward Alex Stahl (Sweden) brings a strong work ethic along with the ability to dribble, pass, and shoot.  6’7 forward Jaylen Jennings (Indianapolis, IN) is an athletic combo forward that gives the team additional versatility with his ability to play inside and outside on the perimeter.  6’2 Josh Holmes (Chicago, IL) is a combo guard that provides play making, shooting and defense. 6’5 forward Jackson Bisungu (United Kingdom) is an athletic combo forward that provides the Cavs with depth. 6’1 guard Kason Williams (Montgomery, AL) gives the team another option at the guard position with his ability to make plays for himself and others.
