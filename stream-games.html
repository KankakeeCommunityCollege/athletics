Indoor Sports

Stream indoor sports on the KCC Athletics YouTube channel.

Baseball

Stream baseball using AWRE:

  1. Go to AWRE.tv and sign up (subscription is $5 monthly.)
  2. Search channels for "Kankakee Community College". You can find past and upcoming games and tournaments.
  3. Stream the games you want on demand.
    There is also AWRE Sports in the App Store and AWRE Sports in the Play Store.

Softball

Stream softball using the GameChanger app:

  1. Download the GameChanger app and create an account if you’re a first-time user.
  2. Then choose “Find Your Team”
  3. Search “KCC Cavaliers” for baseball and “Cavaliers Softball Spring 2024”
  4. Select your relationship – I’m a Fan, I’m a Family Member or I’m a Player
  5. Select “video” to watch a live or recorded game
