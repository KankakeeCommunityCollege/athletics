Stream indoor sports on the KCC Athletics YouTube channel.
Stream baseball and softball using the GameChanger app:

  1. Download the GameChanger app and create an account if you’re a first-time user.
  2. Then choose “Find Your Team”
  3. Search “KCC Cavaliers” for baseball and “Cavaliers Softball Spring 2024”
  4. Select your relationship – I’m a Fan, I’m a Family Member or I’m a Player
  5. Select “video” to watch a live or recorded game
