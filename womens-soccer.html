 Season Preview

The Lady Cavaliers will be taking the field for the first time in its inaugural season.

“We are excited for this opportunity to represent Kankakee Community College and the Athletics Department,” Coach Barclay said. “We have assembled a solid, core group of women to build the foundation of this program for years to come. The high schools and areas soccer clubs we serve at KCC have a long history of producing some top quality women soccer players and we are excited to help these players continue their education and athletic goals here at the collegiate level. We have set the bar high for these ladies in their first season, and they are ready for the challenge!”
