In his first season with the program, Tony Martin brings a deep passion for softball and years of experience on the travel softball circuit. A native of Kankakee, Coach Martin has been both a student of the game and a mentor to young athletes throughout the region. Coach Martin's experience on the travel circuit has given him a strong understanding of player development, team culture, and the commitment it takes to succeed both on and off the field.
